KING KONG
VS
GODZILLA

FIGHTING TO THE DEATH!

Written by Billy Bishilany, Age 7

For every kid who ever wondered...
who would REALLY win.

It was the most PERFECT day in the whole entire ocean.

A cruise ship -- the biggest one EVER BUILT -- carried a million people across the sea. There were pools and waterslides and all-you-can-eat pizza.

Nobody knew what was coming.

Deep, DEEP under the water... something moved.

Something bigger than a whale. Bigger than a submarine. Bigger than ANYTHING.

The ocean started to bubble.

KRRRRAAAASSSSHHH!!!

The ocean EXPLODED -- and out came GODZILLA!

He was taller than the ship. He was taller than a SKYSCRAPER. Water crashed everywhere like the world's biggest cannonball and his roar shook the clouds right out of the sky.

"SKREEEEEONK!!!"

People screamed! People ran! One kid grabbed his pizza and KEPT EATING because it was really good pizza!

WHOOOOSH!

Godzilla swung his tail and waves as tall as buildings smashed across the deck!

The ship tilted sideways. This was NOT on the schedule.

Then -- everyone heard it.

BOOM. BOOM. BOOM.

From a faraway island, trees crashed down like dominoes. The ground shook. Birds flew away screaming.

Even Godzilla stopped... and turned his head.

BA-KOOOM!

KING KONG came flying off the island like a ROCKET!

He landed on a rock so hard it cracked in half and he beat his chest so loud it sounded like THUNDER.

"OOOOH-OOOOH-AAAAHHH!!!"

He looked at Godzilla. Godzilla looked at him.

It. Was. ON.

King Kong PUNCHED Godzilla right in the nose! WHAM!

Godzilla SLAMMED his tail into Kong's legs! CRACK!

Kong grabbed Godzilla's mouth and tried to hold it shut -- but Godzilla's spines started GLOWING and --

FWOOOOOOOOOSH!!!

Atomic breath BLASTED across the ocean like a blue laser beam!

Kong jumped on Godzilla's BACK!

Godzilla spun and thrashed and roared -- but Kong held on like the world's craziest rodeo cowboy!

They wrestled and smashed and crashed until the WHOLE ocean was just waves and foam and CHAOS!

Then... they both went under.

Everything got quiet.

For a long time... nothing.

Then two heads came up from the water. King Kong. And Godzilla. Both still standing.

They looked at each other. They were both tired. They were both tough. They were both... AWESOME.

Kong held out his fist. Godzilla bumped it with his claw.

THE CROWD WENT CRAZY!!!

The cruise ship sailed home. Everyone had the BEST story to tell.

And somewhere out in the ocean, two giants sat together, eating fish and watching the sunset.

Because sometimes the toughest fighters...

...become the toughest FRIENDS.

THE END... OR IS IT?

"I came up with this whole idea myself." -- Billy, Age 7
